The Flexbox layout module in CSS provides a powerful way to align and distribute space among items in a container. It helps developers build responsive designs that adapt seamlessly across screen sizes. Flexbox is especially useful when aligning items horizontally or vertically, or even when you want items spaced out dynamically.
